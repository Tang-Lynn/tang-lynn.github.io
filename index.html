Biography

I am a PhD Candidate at the Department of Methodology & Statistics, Utrecht University, under the supervision of Prof. Herbert Hoijtink and Prof. Xin Gu . Under their guidance, I have been working in the topic of developing Bayesian method for measurement invariance testing, and extending R-package bain, which uses the Bayes factor to evaluate hypotheses specified using equality and inequality constraints among parameters in a wide range of statistical models.
